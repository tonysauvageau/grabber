lag-master is the
professional’s choice

Lag-Master is a heavy-duty, code-compliant wood screw engineered for structural framing jobs and designed to replace 3/8” lag-screws. The type-17 point eliminates pre-drilling and makes for fast installations win even the densest woods. The draw-tite shank pulls trusses and 2 and 3-ply dimensional wood together rather than pushing them apart as the fastener is driven in. The specially designed hex-washer head provides a large bearing area for greater torque and countersinks easily to provide a clean, flush appearance.

Lag-Master is tested and supported by engineering data that conforms to the current ICC standards – ICC-ESR#3531. More Information

best solution
